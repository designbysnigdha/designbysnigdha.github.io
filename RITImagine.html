RIT IMAGINE POSTER

Imagination is like treasure you can find in the depths of sea.

Goals With This Project

I created a poster using Cinema4D. I designed and modeled each underwater element and tried to make it as bright as possible because that is how imagination is. I tried to portray a playful mood while also trying to show how valuable Imagine RIT is as a function.
